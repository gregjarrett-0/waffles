## Usage guidance

The `Paragraph` component offers three different `size` values to choose from: `small`, `medium` and `large`. In most cases, the default `size` should be used.

To highlight the content of importance, use regular `strong` or `em` elements. `Paragraph` also offers a more subtle `variant="secondary`" for use with less important or supportive content. On top of this, others components such as [Code](/components/code) and [Link](/components/link) can be used within the content.

There is also an `inverted` property for cases where `Paragraph` is used on a darker background.

## Best practices

<BestPractices>
  <BestPractices.Recommended>

    - use to improve accessibility&mdash;splitting content into Paragraphs helps screen readers
    - underline content within Paragraph for emphasis&mdash;could be confused with links

  </BestPractices.Recommended>
  <BestPractices.Discouraged>

    - when you need to provide a title or heading&mdash;use a [Heading](/components/heading) instead
    - mix too many different styles within Paragraph

  </BestPractices.Discouraged>
</BestPractices>
