### TextArea

In addition to all custom props listed below, `TextArea` accepts all props regular `HTMLTextArea` would, such as `placeholder`, `disabled`, `rows`, `autocomplete`, `maxLength`, `aria-` and `data-` attributes. Keep in mind that when `value` is specified `onChange` handler must be also provided.
