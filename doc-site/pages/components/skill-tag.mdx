## Usage guidance

A `SkillTag` can be used in course or track description cards and pages to indicate their skill level. It should **never** be used by itself, but rather to provide more visual information and catch the attention of the user.

Use `variant='primary'` when it's positioned near the top of a page or has the intent of catching the eye. In contrast, `variant='secondary'` should be used in cases where the `SkillTag` provides additional supportive information to the surrounding content.
